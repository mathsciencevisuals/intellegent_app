Key Features of ONESOURCE Trust Tax:
Comprehensive Compliance: Prepares and files federal and state tax returns, including Form 1041 (Fiduciary Income Tax), 1041-A, 1041-QFT, 5227, 1040NR, and 3520-A.
Automated Calculations: Handles complex fiduciary tax calculations, including loss limitations and Distributable Net Income (DNI) allocations to beneficiaries.
ONESOURCE Trust Tax Insight: An online portal providing tax stakeholders instant access to return status, allowing on-demand printing of K-1s, 1099s, and reports.
Data Integration: Connects with financial and accounting systems to streamline data transfer and reduce manual entry.
Cloud-Based Workflow: Enables secure, real-time collaboration from any device, simplifying the tax preparation process for trust and estate professionals.
Estate & Gift Tax Support: Offers functionality for Federal 706 (Estate Tax) and 709 (Gift Tax) returns.
